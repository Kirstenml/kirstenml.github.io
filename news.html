Blog post: Green Online Learning with Heterogeneous Ensembles

I wrote a guest article for the Lamarr Institute blog on heterogeneous online ensembles for green stream learning. The post introduces how HEROS enables accurate and resource-efficient learning on data streams under concept drift.

The article discusses why ensembles are useful for stream learning, how heterogeneity can reduce the need for manual hyperparameter tuning, and how the ζ-policy balances predictive performance with resource usage.
